Network Manifesto

Truly ideas are "things", and powerful things when they are combined with a definitness of purpose, persistence, and a burning desire for their translations into reality.

"Anonymous" is such an idea that has been translated into reality. We are the manifestation.

"Anonymous" is defined as "The collective idea of living the philosophy that freedoms are inalienable rights. Anything that opposes freedom is oppressive. Anonymous seeks truth, abhors lies, and teaches truth."

"A mind stretched by a new idea, can never return back to it's smaller state."

"Anonymous" has been incorrectly labeled as a 'hacktivist group', 'terrorist organization' and many more insulting and false labels that do not coincide with reality. Some of us find this.... disturbing. This misinformation is intentional, and has the purpose of turning people away from the idea. There are people who don't want the idea to affect the thoughts of others. By creating lies that make Anonymous appear to be "the bad guy" they hope to hinder, if not completely stop, the spread of truth that is Anonymous. They're kidding themselves. The spreading of this idea is inevitable.

We've written this manifesto due to organized misinformation by the weapons of mass distraction and the ignorant. We've also ensure the propogation of this idea into the minds of the next generation, so that their thoughts may be free and not imprisoned.

Some will ask, "How can I become Anonymous? How do I join?"
My fellow anon, by desiring to support freedom and truth, you already are Anonymous!
May you live free and prosper!
